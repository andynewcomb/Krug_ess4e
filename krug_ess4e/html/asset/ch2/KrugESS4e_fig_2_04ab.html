Figure 2.4: FIGURE 2-4 Production Possibilities for Two Countries
Figure 2.4: Here, both the United States and Brazil have a constant opportunity cost of small jets, illustrated by a straight-line production possibility frontier. For the United States, each small jet has an opportunity cost of 3⁄4 of a large jet. Brazil has an opportunity cost of a small jet equal to 1⁄3 of a large jet.
